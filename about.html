About Me

I’m a certified degen who treats the world like a sandbox to be stripped for parts: I pilot overpriced ships in Star Citizen not to conquer but to collect, turn Minecraft landscapes into deliberate eyesores, and punish my digits in osu! until something gives. These aren’t hobbies so much as refined distractions — I watch civilization fray and pick the best loot off its skeleton, then hang neon over the mess because aesthetics are the only decent cruelty left.

I devour books like contraband in a blackout — rapid, greedy, and with a running commentary of spoilers I whisper at strangers. Audiobooks are my alibi: hand on controller, mind on elegy; I can clear a salvage run and finish a bleak little chapter before the kettle boils. Plots lie less than people, and that’s the kind of loyalty I can respect.

Yes, I like ecchi anime — the shameless angles, the ridiculous rules of engagement, the memes — it’s tasteful in the exact way a dumpster fire is cozy. Music is the score for the slow-motion undoing: adrenaline for conflict, ambient for pretending to be productive, and crushing beats for when osu! makes my brain crack.
